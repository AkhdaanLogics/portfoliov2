## Overview

My Notetify is a fun and creative web application that connects to your Spotify account and generates beautiful note-style receipts of your top songs. Inspired by the popular Receiptify concept, this app showcases your music taste in an elegant and shareable format that looks like a receipt from a music store.

<ProjectSlider />

## Key Features

- **Spotify Integration**: Seamlessly authenticate and access your Spotify listening data
- **Top Songs Display**: View your most listened tracks over different time periods (short, medium, long term)
- **Receipt-Style Design**: Creative note/receipt layout that mimics a music store receipt
- **Customizable Views**: Toggle between different time ranges (last 4 weeks, last 6 months, all-time)
- **Share Ready**: Generate beautiful images that are perfect for social media sharing
- **Responsive Design**: Works flawlessly on all devices from mobile to desktop
- **Artist Information**: Display artist names, song duration, and album artwork
- **Dark Theme**: Modern dark UI with excellent contrast and readability

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: React
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **API**: Spotify Web API
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## How It Works

1. **Spotify Login**: Users authenticate via Spotify OAuth to securely access their account
2. **Data Fetching**: The app retrieves the user's top tracks from Spotify's API
3. **Receipt Generation**: Songs are formatted in a creative receipt-style layout
4. **Visual Design**: Each song displays with artist info, duration, and album artwork
5. **Export Ready**: Users can screenshot or download their music receipt

## Design Inspiration

The design is inspired by Receiptify but with a unique twist - creating elegant note-like receipts that feel nostalgic and fun. The layout mimics an actual receipt with:

- Receipt-style headers and footers
- Listed items (songs) with details
- Total count and time period information
- Clean typography and spacing
